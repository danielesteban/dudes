Welcome, my dudes!

This is an alpha version of my new voxels engine: "Dudes!".
The backend is written in C (compiled to WASM with LLVM) and it has the usual stuff plus 3D pathfinding and physics.

It features 4 demo scenes:

  • A helicopter gameplay where you help "The Chief" get dudes up to the terrace party.
  • Same party without the helicopter business (composability!).
  • Some state-of-the-art poop tech.
  • And a scene to debug the voxel building tools, the physics contact callbacks and the dudes pathfinding.

I also plan to release the full source. I just haven't finished cleaning it up and also keep adding stuff like there's no tomorrow. But I promise it will eventually happen haha.
